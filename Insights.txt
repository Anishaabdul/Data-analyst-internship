Insight 1 – Top Region

The West region contributes the highest share of total sales (around 38%), making it the strongest revenue-generating region. This suggests higher customer demand and better market penetration in this region.

Insight 2 – Best Category

The Technology category records the highest total sales, indicating strong customer preference and growth potential compared to other categories.

Insight 3 – Segment Performance

The Consumer segment generates the maximum sales volume, showing it is the most important customer segment for overall business performance.

Insight 4 – Profitability Insight

Despite high sales, some categories show lower profit margins, highlighting the need to control costs or revise pricing strategies to improve profitability.

Insight 5 – Business Opportunity

Regions with lower sales contribution present an opportunity for targeted marketing and promotional campaigns to boost overall revenue.
